Who We Are

YORA TECHNOLOGIES is a proprietary firm in manufacturing a trading of heating ventilation & air-conditioning and refrigeration (HVAC& R) products and equipment. Our manufacturing unit and office are based in Ghaziabad, U.P.

The Firm was established in 2011 with the experience of more than 22 years of experience in similar industry.

YORA TECHNOLOGIES offers a vast wealth of experience in manufacturing and product development, ensuring customers requirement and satisfaction.

A current range of products comprises of :

  • Cabinet Inline Fans - single phase direct driven (1000 – 6500cfm)
  • Compact Fan section (Fresh air, exhaust & Ventilation )- belt driven (1000 – 20000cfm)
  • Circular Inline fans (Make: PINEAIR & Ostberg) - (90-900cfm)
  • Grills & Diffusers
  • Damper
  • Thermal Insulation (Open cell & Close cell- Brand Armacell, K- flex, ALP)
  • Flexible duct.
  • Filters (Pre-EU4, Fine & Bag-EU5 & 7 and Hepa EU-9 & 13.)
  • Accessories like Plenums, Motor Base Plate, Vibration Isolators, VRV Cowls & Spigots.
  • Sheet Metal & Structural work.
  • Refrigeration and air conditioning oil and all related tools etc.

We also deal in products of SWEP, Denfoss, Henry, Bitzer& Timeline.
